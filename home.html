Wind Energy Solutions

Harnessing the Power of Wind

Wind energy is one of the fastest-growing renewable energy technologies. Our solutions leverage cutting-edge turbine technology to convert wind's kinetic energy into clean electricity. Modern wind turbines are highly efficient, with capacities ranging from 2-3 MW for onshore installations to 8-12 MW for offshore projects.

Technical Implementation

Our wind energy systems use advanced control algorithms to optimize performance:

// Sample wind turbine control algorithm
function adjustBladePitch(windSpeed) {
    if (windSpeed < 3) return 0;  // Below cut-in speed
    if (windSpeed > 25) return 90; // Feather blades for safety
    return Math.min(15, windSpeed * 0.6); // Optimal pitch angle
}
